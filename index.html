Garlic Parmesan Asparagus

Ingredients you will need:

  • 1 Bundle of Asparagus
  • 3 Tbsp. Butter
  • 3 Tbsp. Minced Garlic
  • 1/3 cup Parmesan Cheese
  • Dash of Seasoning Salt (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Pre-heat oven to 400 degrees.
  2. In a small saucepan, melt butter over medium heat.
  3. Add garlic to melted butter and simmer on low for 2 minutes.
  4. In a baking dish, pour garlic butter over asparagus and coat evenly.
  5. Coat evenly with Parmesan cheese.
  6. Bake for 15-18 mins until cheese is lightly golden.
