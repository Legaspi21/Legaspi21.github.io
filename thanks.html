Guacamole

Ingredients you will need:

  • 4 Ripe Avocados
  • 1 Bunch Cilantro
  • 1 Jalapeno Pepper
  • 2 Cloves Garlic
  • 1/2 Onion
  • 1 Lime
  • 3 Tomatoes
  • 1 Tsp. Salt
  • 1/2 Tsp. Pepper

Instructions:

  1. Chop cilantro, jalapeno, garlic and onion.
  2. Combine above ingredients in a mixing bowl and add lime juice, salt and pepper.
  3. Mash everything together with a fork or molcajete.
  4. Add avocado.
  5. Mash together.
  6. Add chopped tomatoes.
  7. Garnish with cilantro and chips.
  8. Enjoy!
